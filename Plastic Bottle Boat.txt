<h4 class="pic1Title sub-imageTitle">Spreading the Word on Sustainability</h2>
<p class="pic1Discription" style="width: 275px;">The concept and focus throughout this unit is sustainability and the effect of non-biodegradable materials on the environment.  When we go to the beach with our boats we inevitably get lots of questions.  Students decided to place an informational display up that explains what and why we are there.</p>
</div>

<div style="width: 152px; top: 20px; float: left; background-color: white; left: 25px;">

<h4 class="pic2Title sub-imageTitle">Reusing Materials</h2>
<p class="pic2Discription" style="width: 145px;">Throughout the year students gather random "trash" along with plastic bottles and use it to design and construct a well functioning boat.  Past designs have included bottles, bottle labels, paper roll tubes, old PVC pipes, planks from 4-foot subs, old palletes, and more.</p>
</div>

<div style="width: 152px; top: 20px; float: left; background-color: white; left: 50px;">

<h4 class="pic3Title sub-imageTitle">Different Designs</h2>
<p class="pic3Discription" style="width: 145px;">Each class works collaboratively to come up with the "best" design.  Some classes elect to go for a sleek fast design inspired by a surf board or kayak, while other prefer the stability that a pantoon or raft offers.</p>
</div>
